About this Site

The site is designed for searching and comparing scriptures and related ancient texts from around the world. All texts are stored in English, organized by book, chapter and verse.

Choose Collections and Translations

Use the 'Advanced Filter' link at the top to select the volumes and translations of interest.

Types of Searches

  • enter a word, phrase or group of words that are of interest
  • enter a query in Book:Chapter:Verse (E.g., Quran:1:1, Genesis:2:2, 1 Peter:5:1) format to locate a specific verse
  • within a set of results, choose the scroll icon () next to any verse to look for similar texts
